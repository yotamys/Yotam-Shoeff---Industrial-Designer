Brief

Choose a specific space and users. Design a new electric micromobility vehicle tailored to its unique needs.

Project Overview

ICON allows comfortable and effortless movement between exhibits. Instead of a long walk around the museum with occasional sitting breaks, users can remain seated and relaxed throughout the entire tour, standing only when reaching an exhibit. This redefines the museum experience, a complete shift from the traditional visit, ensuring a fatigue-free experience.

ICON is designed with a compact body to fit seamlessly in museum spaces. It features an automatic navigation system for easy touring and a customizable route for a personalized experience. The seat is designed for easy sitting and standing with adjustable height. Built-in safety systems protect both users and exhibits, ensuring smooth and secure operation in sensitive environments.

Modeling

For the purpose of physical modeling, a CAD file was prepared and optimized for 3D printing. Following the print, extensive sanding, finishing, and painting work was carried out to achieve a final model that accurately reflects the intended colors and material appearance defined in the original design.
