Hammer Case

Brief Overview

Brief

Choose a profession in which hammers are used. Design a new or improved tool for this profession.

Project Overview

This was a quick two-week warm-up project.
Geologist Hammer was developed to address two common issues in traditional tools: excessive weight and rapid wear of the hammerhead. The design leverages a 3D-printed titanium structure based on a Voronoi diagram, significantly reducing weight while enhancing durability and material strength. In addition to the primary striking surface, the hammer features two auxiliary edges designed to allow for more controlled shaping and fragmentation of stone samples in the field.

Two subtle graphic lines on the head help visually guide the user to these additional edges, supporting intuitive and efficient use. The result is a lightweight, long-lasting tool tailored to the practical needs of geologists working in demanding environments.
