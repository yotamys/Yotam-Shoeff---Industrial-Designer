Pen Case

Brief Overview

Brief

Design a product based on laser cutting technology and its properties.

Project Overview

Pen Case was designed as a practical solution for architects and engineers working on construction sites, where expensive stationery is often exposed to harsh conditions. The case is cut from a single piece of fabric using a 2D layout that folds into itself and connects at a single point to form an internal hanger, eliminating the need for stitching or seams.

The layout is carefully designed to minimize material waste while remaining compact and easy to carry. The chosen fabric tends to fray when cut with scissors, but laser cutting melts the edges, sealing them cleanly and eliminating the issue entirely. The final design was refined through prototyping and material testing to ensure durability, ease of use, and efficient production.
