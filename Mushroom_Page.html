Brief

Design a new tool for a specific food industry. Chosen industry - food processing and growing.

Project Overview

This ceramic Mushrooms Growing Pot was designed to offer a safe, user-friendly alternative for home growers, addressing the lack of proper tools and the challenges of maintaining a consistently moist environment. The pot features a unique one-sided glazed ceramic body paired with an airtight, dual-function silicone lid. Its material structure allows for moisture regulation: the unglazed inner side is designed to absorb water when wetted, gradually releasing humidity to create an ideal microclimate for mushroom growth. The glazed outer surface, combined with the silicone lid, helps prevent evaporation, reducing the need for frequent watering.

The pot is also stackable, making it suitable for compact indoor spaces. This design offers an accessible, elegant solution that supports healthy mushroom cultivation without relying on improvised or potentially unsafe setups.
